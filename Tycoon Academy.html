Tycoon International Football Academy is one of the best private football academy, providing football courses alongside academic studies for aspiring footballers who are aged 16 or older. The courses we offer range from one week to three years and combine specialist football training with first-class education. The quality of the academy has seen former students succeeding at professional football clubs within the UK, Europe, USA or at a significantly higher level in their home country, with many going on to represent their national teams.




Tycoon football academy is a co-educational day and boarding school, set across two beautiful sites in Ontario, and Virginia. Through our Football Academy, we offer an intensive football development programme capable of taking the most gifted footballers to the next level as well as opening doors to a range of roles in the game such as coaching, scouting, performance analysis and research. To succeed in today’s professional game, it is important to work hard both on the pitch and in the classroom. Tycoon football academy is rated Outstanding by the government regulator (OFSTED) and all Academy students follow a structured and rigorous programme of coaching and academic study. We believe that our experienced coaching team has created a unique and dynamic play and study programme, capable of enabling all young players to realise their footballing dreams and fulfil their academic potential.With clear progression routes, opportunities to play alongside current Premier League academy players already at the School, and to compete against professional clubs at professional football club grounds, talented and aspiring players are not only recognised, but tested and pushed so that they quickly improve their game and skills.
